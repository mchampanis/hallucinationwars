# AI use
Some of the code here was written with AI assistance (specifically Claude
by Anthropic). We directed it, reviewed it, and we're responsible for it.
Saying otherwise would be dishonest. We have enough experience to know
when the output is good and when it isn't, and to push it towards
something worth shipping. The ideas are our own - the work is genuinely
collaborative, but probably not groundbreaking.

## Resource cost
Running AI uses real electricity and water. Those costs land
on communities near data centers and on the climate. We can't offset that
individually, but we're not going to pretend it isn't happening.

## Training data
The models we use were probably trained on a mix of work that
was shared freely and work that wasn't. That's a real problem with no clean
individual fix. Being upfront about using the tools is the best we can do.

## Unethical means, ethical ends
Using something with a known cost to do
something useful doesn't erase the cost - both things are just true. We've
weighed it and chosen to proceed.

## Content concerns
If you think your work ended up in a model I used, or that something here
reproduces your work without credit, please open an issue.

## Discussion
We are happy to discuss any of the above.
